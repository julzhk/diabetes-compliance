Never forget your meds ever again!

Reminders to take your diabetes medicine: easily, daily

Do you ever miss your diabetes medications? You are not alone!

About 50% of all people taking regular medications miss some of the doses. As a result sufferers are more likely to have complications of diabetes such as: heart, kidney, eye and nerve problems.
Mimi provides you with a daily reminder and encourages you by playing interactive games and quizzes every day - if you've taken your meds!
Simple as that!

Meet Mimi!

Mimi is an autonomous chatbot that uses natural language processing and games to gently remind you to take your daily diabetes medication.
Mimi is a Chatbot, so she can hold a natural conversation with a human - answering questions about Diabetes, or how well you are keeping your schedule, or even keeping a diary for you.
Every day she provides you with your daily reminder and to encourage you, she plays games, setting quizzes so you can learn more about Diabetes, as well as other activities.

Mimi is smart!

Mimi is smart, and has a friendly encouraging personality - she wants you to take your medicines and rewards you if you do!
She uses your usual message platform (currently just Facebook messenger and text messages but others to follow soon) to make it easy to get her reminders and to converse with her.

How does this help you?

Keep track of your medicines

  • Reminds you to take your meds, at a time convenient for you
  • Rewards you by playing games, or quizzes if you succeed!
  • Communicates with you where you communicate: in your favourite messaging platform

Improve knowledge

  • Knowing about your diabetes is important - and Mimi can define jargon and provide information
  • Mimi will periodically quiz you on your knowledge - scoring points and learning new things.
  • Use Mimi to remember events - tracking your mood, activities, and other relevant details whenever you like!

Mimi plays games

  • Encouraging your success by giving rewards
  • Each successful game provides a chance to score points: solve a puzzle, win a game, answer a quiz: all give you a boost!
  • She tracks your progress, letting you compete with friends, and share your improvements with others.
